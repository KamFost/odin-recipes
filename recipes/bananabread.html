Description

Banana bread is a family favorite and easy to bake and serve

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas, mashed
  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • eggs
  • sour cream or yogurt, to taste

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. mash your bananas if you have not already done so
  3. combine bananas with butter, eggs, and sour cream or yogurt
  4. mix in brown sugar a little at a time until you have a nice dough.
  5. bake at 350 for 45 minutes
  6. allow to cool before slicing
