Chocochip Cookies

Description

This is a recipe for classic chocochip cookies, just like grandma used to make! These cookies can be made by anyone at any expertise level and are loved by people the world over.

We recommend not skimping on the butter and using farm fresh eggs for the best results. Children should consider asking an adult for help.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Flour
  • Sea Salt
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees and allow butter to soften to room temperature.
  2. Combine dry ingredients. Use a sifter to mix well.
  3. Beat butter and eggs until well combined.
  4. Stir dry ingredients into the butter and egg mixture a little at a time. Beat until well mixed.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips until well dispersed.
  6. Cook at 350 degrees for 20 minutes until golden brown. Be sure to let them cool!
