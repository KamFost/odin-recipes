Nice Cream

Description

Nice cream is a great replacement for traditional ice cream because it's much healthier and easy to make with just a few ingredients. You can make banana nice cream with just bananas, chocolate nice cream by adding a little coacoa powder, or you can really experiment and try many different combinations to find your new favorite frozen treat.

Ingredients

  1. Frozen banana slices
  2. Mix-ins such as peanbut butter, chocolate, or other fruit.

Steps

  1. Freeze your bananas before making nice cream. It's best to cut them up into small slices and then freeze them over night or longer.
  2. Add your banana slices to a blender or nice cream machine and beat until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add in your additional flavors or toppings and mix until well blended.
  4. Enjoy! You can even save any left overs in the freezer for later.
